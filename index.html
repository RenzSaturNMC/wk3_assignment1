Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. J's Restaurant, Gualo Rai, Saipan
  2. Shirley's Restaurant
  3. Ocean Star Restaurant
  4. Garlic Grotto
  5. Wild Bill's

J's Restaurant

J's Restaurant has been my family's go to restaurant for many years. It has many favorite local dishes. From fried rice and bulalalo to chop suey and soba, the comfort food is plentiful and is waiting to be enjoyed by you! J's also have a small arcade area nearby, so you can entertain yourself after a delectable meal

What I enjoy in J's:

  • Chicken Tocino Fried Rice
  • Salisbury Steak
  • Bulalo
  • Family-centric atmosphere
