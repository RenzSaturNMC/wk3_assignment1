Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. J's Restaurant
  2. Shirley's Restaurant
  3. Ocean Star Restaurant
  4. Garlic Grotto
  5. Wild Bill's

J's Restaurant | Gualo Rai, Saipan

J's Restaurant has been my family's go to restaurant for many years. It has many favorite local dishes. From fried rice and bulalo to chop suey and soba, the comfort food is plentiful and is waiting to be enjoyed by you! J's also have a small arcade area nearby, so you can entertain yourself after a delectable meal


What I enjoy in J's:

  • Chicken Tocino Fried Rice
  • Salisbury Steak
  • Bulalo
  • Family-centric atmosphere

Shirley's Restaurant | Garapan, Saipan

Shirley's was previously my family's go to restaurant for many years. However, we still dine in whenever we can. The fried rice is one of my favorites in this island. The decorations are lined with gorgeous pop art murals. The restaurant seems modern but comfy. The menu is filled with dishes that will soon become your favorites.

What I enjoy in Shirley's:

  • "The Home of the Best Fried Rice"
  • Promoting eco-friendly options
  • Cool Art Murals

Ocean Star | Garapan, Saipan

Ocean Star is a small restaurant, but the food is great. It features a small private room where you can host parties with your family or friends.


What I enjoy in Ocean Star:

  • Crispy Kare Kare
  • Variations of Fried Rice
  • Karaoke Room for private parties
